How to Survive a Quarantine

Stock Up on Supplies
IE "Toilet Paper"
  1. Stock Up On Food
  2. Wash Your Hands
  3. Social Distance
  4. Disinifect commonly touched objects
  5. Stay Inside as much as possible

When you have to go to the store be sure to clean your hands before you go in and aftewards.

If you order food have it delivered and empty the bags to a new plate or serving tray. Be sure to wash your hands and throw away the bags. If you save the food store them in tupperware containers and not the boxes they were delivered in.

Binge Watch

  • Netflix
  • Hulu

  • Amazon Video
  • Youtube TV

Almost everyone now adays watches most of their TV Shows and Movies via some sort of a streaming service. Each service has original content that other services don't have. As such it can benefit you if you subscribe to each one and is still cheaper than cable.

A good set up has a monitor or two, a printer, a desk, a chair, paper and pens.

Work from Home
Set Up Your Workspace.

Many companies give their employees the option to work from home. With COVID19 going on many people are required to stay home. Many companies are closed all together but many companies are still able to do their work from home. To be sufficient it is important to have a seperate space to be able to focus and to set up the required components.
